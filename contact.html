    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find answers to common questions about our services and facilities.

    How do I book an appointment at MediCare Hospital?

    You can book an appointment by calling our appointment line at (234)07014519794, through our website's appointment form, or by visiting any of our branches in person. Online booking is available 24/7 for your convenience.

    What health insurance plans do you accept?

    We accept most major health insurance plans in Nigeria including NHIS, Hygeia HMO, AXA Mansard, Avon HMO, and many others. Please contact our billing department at billing@medicarehospital.ng to verify if your specific plan is accepted.

    What should I bring for my first appointment?

    For your first appointment, please bring:

    • Valid identification (National ID, Drivers License, or International Passport)

    • Health insurance card (if applicable)
    • List of current medications
    • Medical records or test results related to your condition
    • Medical records or test results related to your condition
    • Payment method for any copayments or deductibles
    Do you have emergency services?

    Yes, all our branches have 24/7 emergency departments staffed with qualified emergency physicians and equipped with state-of-the-art facilities. In case of emergency, please call (234) 812 345 6789 or proceed directly to the nearest MediCare Hospital emergency room.

    How can I access my medical records?

    You can request your medical records by visiting our medical records department at any of our branches with valid identification. Alternatively, you can download our patient portal app "MediCare Connect" where you can securely access your health information, test results, and appointment history.
