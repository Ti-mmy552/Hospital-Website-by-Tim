    • Our Services and Specialist

    .

    Oncology

    The current rise in incidence of cancer in young and old is alarming. Our team of experts focuses on the diagnosis, assessment and treatment of various types of cancer, as well as your health needs beyond cancer.

    Diagnostic

    At HealthWave Progress our modern laboratory is equipped to carry out hematological, biochemical and immunological investigations. In our radiology department, we are able to carry out x-rays and ultrasound scans with accurate reportage to ensure correct diagnosis. Other diagnostic procedures we carry out include; Electrocardiograms; Spirometry (an evaluation of lung function); Pap smears (to check for cervical cancer); We perform Urinalyses; Urine Cultures; Throat cultures; Haemoglobin tests (for detection of anaemia); Blood glucose tests; Pregnancy tests; Stool occult blood tests and other selected tests.

    Women's Health

    We offer care and support to help women manage their health at every stage of life. Our caring staff offer a wide range of services, including primary care, family planning, prenatal through delivery care, midwifery services, postpartum care, breastfeeding/lactation services, breast exams, nutrition, preventative care, vaccinations, and more.

    Antenatal Clinic

    We have regular ante natal classes to suit your time and needs. Post-natal Each patient has a six-week appointment after baby is born to ensure that mummy and baby(ies) are doing well.

    Baby Bumb Classes

    During the period of your pregnancy for first time parents and experienced parents we have regular, fun and informative antenatal classes. Mummy and daddy are invited.

    Gynecologic Clinic

    Our Gynecologists are skillful, caring, and have years of experience in and out of theatre.

    Women HAC

    We have specific health screens for women to prevent and detect illnesses. These have been put together in a stress-free package for every woman.

    Cardiology

    Comprehensive heart care including diagnostics, treatment, and rehabilitation for all cardiac conditions. Advanced diagnostics, interventional procedures, and rehabilitation programs with our state-of-the-art cardiac center.

    Neurology

    Expert care for disorders of the nervous system, including stroke, epilepsy, and Parkinson's disease. Specialized care for neurological disorders including stroke management, epilepsy treatment, and movement disorder therapies with our expert neurologists.

    Orthopaedic

    Treatment for musculoskeletal injuries and conditions, including joint replacements and sports medicine. Advanced treatments for musculoskeletal conditions including robotic joint replacements, sports injuries, and spine care with our orthopedic specialists.

    Radiology

    Advanced imaging services including MRI, CT scans, ultrasound, and X-rays for accurate diagnosis.

    Surgery

    At HealthWave Progress hospital, we have two fully equipped theatres and trained theatre practitioners, technicians, nurses, surgeons and anaesthetists who ensure that cases are done safely and efficiently. We are fully aware that “safe surgery saves lives”

    Urological Surgery

    Our in-house urologist provides treatment for a variety of concerns including, but not limited to, Prostate Cancer, bladder cancer, renal cancer, impotence, kidney stone and infertility. We also carry out Procedures such as vasectomy and laparoscopic surgeries.

    General Surgery

    From upper gastrointestinal to lower gastrointestinal surgery to breast surgery and thyroid surgeries our team of trained surgeons provide the skills and technique necessary for each case for each patient.

    Maxillofacial Surgery

    The Maxillofacial surgeon provides a comprehensive range of services to correct problems with the face, mouth, teeth and jaws.
    These problems include;

    • head and neck cancers

    • facial trauma, both hard and soft tissue
    • facial deformity
    • reconstructive surgery of the mouth and jaws
    • surgical dermatology for face and neck cancers
    • minor oral surgery

    Nephrology

    The prevalence of kidney disease is rising globally and in Nigeria. In response to this we at Marigold Hospital and Critical Care Centre have a well-equipped dialysis unit manned by a nephrologist and a team of dialysis nurses, technicians and doctors. Our aim is to provide accessible and affordable dialysis for in patients and out patients.
